OUR STORY

A place for the people around us.

Pillars was created to serve the people who work, build and create within Solaris at Fusionopolis.

Whether it is the first coffee of the morning or a quick afternoon reset, we believe great coffee should feel dependable, welcoming and easy to return to.

COFFEE FIRST

Consistent, clean and approachable.

Our menu changes throughout the year as coffees rotate, but our approach remains the same — clean, balanced and approachable coffees prepared with consistency and care.

We focus on coffees that are enjoyable daily while still showcasing the character of their origin.
